DeLonghi Stilosa Review 2026 – Is It Worth Buying?

The DeLonghi Stilosa is one of the most popular entry-level espresso machines for home baristas. If you're looking for an affordable espresso machine that can produce real espresso with proper pressure, this model is often recommended as a beginner-friendly option.

In this detailed review, we’ll analyze the features, build quality, espresso performance, milk frothing capability, ease of use, pros and cons, and who this machine is best suited for.

Main Features

  • 15-bar pressure pump
  • Manual milk frother
  • Stainless steel boiler
  • Compact and modern design
  • Affordable price range

Build Quality & Design

The DeLonghi Stilosa features a compact and lightweight design that fits easily in small kitchens. While the exterior includes plastic components, the internal pump system delivers consistent pressure for proper espresso extraction.

The stainless steel accents give it a premium look despite its affordable price. It is available in multiple colors, making it suitable for different kitchen aesthetics.

Espresso Performance

One of the biggest advantages of the Stilosa is its 15-bar pressure pump. This level of pressure is important for extracting rich espresso with proper crema.

When paired with freshly ground coffee and correct tamping technique, the machine can produce surprisingly good espresso shots for its price category.

However, keep in mind that this machine does not include a built-in grinder. For best results, using a quality burr grinder is recommended.

Milk Frothing Capability

The manual milk frother allows you to create cappuccinos and lattes. While it requires some practice, users can achieve decent microfoam after a few attempts.

Since it is a manual steam wand, it provides more control compared to automatic frothing systems, but beginners may need time to master it.

Ease of Use

The control panel is simple and beginner-friendly. There are no complex digital screens or advanced programming options, making it ideal for users who prefer straightforward operation.

Cleaning is relatively easy, and the removable drip tray helps maintain hygiene.

Pros

  • Very affordable entry-level machine
  • Proper 15-bar pressure pump
  • Compact size
  • Good espresso quality for the price

Cons

  • No built-in grinder
  • Plastic components
  • Manual frother requires practice

Who Is It For?

The DeLonghi Stilosa is ideal for beginners who want to start making espresso at home without investing in an expensive professional machine.

If you're new to espresso and willing to learn basic techniques like tamping and milk frothing, this machine offers excellent value.

However, advanced users who want precise temperature control or built-in grinders may prefer higher-end models.

Final Verdict

Overall, the DeLonghi Stilosa delivers strong value for money. It is not a professional-grade espresso machine, but for beginners and casual users, it provides everything needed to start brewing real espresso at home.

If you're searching for the best espresso machine for beginners under a reasonable budget, the Stilosa is a solid and reliable option.
