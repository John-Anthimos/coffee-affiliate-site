Buying Guide: What to Look For

When choosing a coffee grinder, consider burr type, grind settings, motor power, and ease of cleaning. Burr grinders are generally superior to blade grinders because they provide consistent particle size.

If you brew espresso, you need a grinder with very fine and precise adjustments. For French press or drip coffee, medium to coarse settings are sufficient.
